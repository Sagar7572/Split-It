About Us

Splitting your bills or expenses may be a very tedious job sometimes.
Whether it’s about sharing living expenses with flatmates or hanging out
together with friends managing expenses isn’t a cup of tea for everyone.
From awkward reminders to delayed payments people normally face such issues
when it’s about sharing expenses with friends or colleagues.
Therefore we need something that gets the work done and saves us from any troubles in money matters. This project aims to build an app just to serve that purpose; it makes the process of managing group expenses super easy.
Seamlessly Keep track of your shared expenses and balances with housemates, trips, groups, friends, and family. Less stress and less awkwardness.
Split IT is an application software which is used in tracking the financial expenditure of a group of people spending capital on common expenses which must be equally divided in the group we focus on settlement in a fair manner. Settlement of bills and common commodities such that the honour of debt is kept.
